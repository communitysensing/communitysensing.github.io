Related

Common Sense is a collaborative research project that builds in part on the N-SMARTS and Urban Atmospheres research projects.

We are not alone in exploring mobile participatory sensing. Projects working in the area of mobile, network-connected sensing platforms for public data collection include:

  • AIR and Pigeonblog (Preemptive Media)
  • CarTel (MIT CSAIL)
  • MetroSense (Dartmouth)
  • Mobile Urban Sensing (Cambridge)
  • Sensor Planet (Nokia)
  • SenseWeb (Microsoft Research)
  • Urban Sensing (UCLA CENS)

A few of the many projects that capture public geo-referenced environmental data sets include:

  • Bucket Brigade (Global Community Monitor)
  • Urban Pollution Monitoring (Equator)
